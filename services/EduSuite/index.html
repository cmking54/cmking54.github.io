EduSuite

Welcome to Intergrated Lecture Interaction

EduSuite is a tool meant for both sides of a lecture. Whether presenting to a stadium or a classroom, this tool will allow for quick and live call-respond making public speaking a breeze.

Sign Up Sign In

EduSuite will handle file upload and apply an interactive layer so interaction is recorded and sent to the speaker.

Now in the works, a dynamic implementation where the functionality will work over a video, record input, and send the inputs to the teacher. Perfect for online lessons and reverse classrooms!
